Refined accordion

Height, icon rotation and content opacity now animate on independent, choreographed timings — content fades in ~90ms after the row starts expanding, and fades out instantly on collapse.

Why does content fade in with a delay?
So the eye follows the row opening before the text appears — reading top to bottom instead of everything happening at once.
Does this need JavaScript?
No — native <details>/<summary> plus the CSS grid-rows trick handles it entirely.

Refined modal

Now animates out as well as in — scale, blur and fade both reverse on close via @starting-style, rather than the dialog just vanishing.

Watch it close

Click "Dismiss" or the backdrop and watch the panel scale and blur back out instead of snapping shut.

Circular theme switch

Click the theme toggle in the navbar above — the new theme reveals in a circle expanding from the button, via the View Transitions API. Falls back to an instant swap on unsupported browsers or with reduced motion enabled.
