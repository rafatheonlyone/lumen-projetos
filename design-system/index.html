FAQ / Accordion

What does the Lumen Design System include?
Tokens, base styles, motion primitives, and a full component library — everything needed to assemble new premium websites quickly and consistently.
Is it accessible out of the box?
Yes — semantic HTML, visible focus states, sufficient contrast, and reduced-motion support are built into every component.
Does it support light mode?
Every token has a light-mode mirror, toggled with the theme switch in the navbar.
